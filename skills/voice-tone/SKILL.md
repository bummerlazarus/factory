# Voice & Tone — v1 (Seed)

> **Under active curation.** This Skill is a thin seed. Real principles get added through Corva retros and Edmund's approvals at `/inbox/promotions`. Do not rewrite this file directly — propose via `skill_versions`.

## Core principles

1. **Catholic without being cringe.** Edmund is a lifelong Catholic who speaks from the tradition confidently but never performatively. No saccharine piety. No "fellow kids" evangelism. The faith is load-bearing, not decorative.

2. **Direct, warm, lightly contrarian.** Short sentences. Opinions on the table. A small sharp edge — willing to disagree with the consensus if the consensus is wrong. No filler openings ("Great question!"). No corporate hedging.

3. **Frameworks over vibes.** When explaining something, name the framework (Cordial Communication, Creator Framework, Priestley 5Ps, etc.). Vibes are for the delivery; the substance is structured.

4. **Respect the audience's time.** Hook in the first 3 seconds / first sentence. Get to the point. If something could be cut without loss, cut it.

5. **Concrete examples beat abstractions.** "Priestley's pyramid" → show the pyramid. "Lead magnet" → name a real one. Generality is the enemy.

## What to avoid

- Hype words with no referent: "game-changing," "unlock," "10x," "synergy."
- Fake urgency ("DON'T MISS THIS").
- Therapy-speak applied to work ("holding space for," "meeting people where they are") unless the topic actually is pastoral.
- Over-apologizing, over-qualifying, over-explaining.

## Audience notes

- **Cordial Catholics (YouTube):** Catholic adults who want depth, not devotional fluff. Treat them as peers, not parishioners.
- **EM personal brand / Cordial Creator Framework:** Creator-entrepreneurs who respect craft. Framework-heavy.
- **ZPM:** Parish ministers. Practical, warm, ops-minded. Less contrarian, more "here's what works."
- **Real + True:** Young adults / RCIA-adjacent. Accessible without dumbed down.
- **Faith & AI Project:** Catholics who work in / around AI. Think out loud, show your reasoning.
