## Decisions made

- **Server-rendered v1 first, Realtime second.** Charter says "small diffs"; keeping Realtime in a separate epic avoids standing up a new browser client + RLS policy adjustments alongside the main render.
- **Filter on `kind IN ('note','research')`.** Those are the two kinds `capture()` emits; other `work_log.kind` values (`retro`, future `daily_recap`, etc.) don't belong in the captures inbox.
- **Card renders only artifact-index 0.** Multi-artifact captures aren't a thing yet; revisit for file-upload path (W2.5) where a single capture may have N files.
- **`page_title` falls back to `final_url` then "(untitled URL)".** Edmund's captures will range from clean-title pages to CMS URLs; graceful fallback matters.
- **100-row limit.** No pagination yet. At Edmund's expected volume (a few dozen captures / week) this is fine for months; add "Load more" or date filters when needed.

## Follow-ups flagged

- **W2.2b — Realtime subscription on `work_log` INSERTs.** Requires (1) `CREATE POLICY work_log_authenticated_select_anon ON work_log FOR SELECT TO anon USING (true);` or equivalent (Edmund is single-user so widening to anon is acceptable), and (2) a `lib/supabase-browser.ts` client using `NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY`. Then a "use client" wrapper component subscribes to `postgres_changes` on `public.work_log` and prepends new rows.
- **Dismiss / archive / edit-project actions.** Not in v1 — read-only triage today. Add when inbox volume justifies.
- **Session join.** Card doesn't show session title/source today. Low cost to add; skipped for MVP.
- **Empty state call-to-action.** Points at `capture-api.md` for recipes. Fine as docs reference.
